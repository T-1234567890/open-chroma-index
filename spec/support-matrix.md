# Support Matrix

The support matrix reports the status of exporting canonical OKLCH into target
systems.

Statuses:
- supported
- lossy
- gamut_mapped
- approximation
- unsupported
- profile_required
- proof_required
- user_supplied_reference

For v1-beta, out-of-gamut colors are marked unsupported when no gamut mapping is
implemented. CMYK is profile_required. Pantone and RAL are not implemented.
